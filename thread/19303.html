#1 Recruiting members to RIF Sports Commission 作者：欧艾沃 发表时间：2013-3-13 1:55:31

RIF Sports Commission was established in 1997 by the General Assembly. The main task of the Sports Commission is to publish information about renju tournaments all over the world.
It also discusses about possible rule changes for RIF title tournaments. 
The current members are:
Aivo Oll (chairman) and Ants Soosõrv from Estonia, Cao Dong from China, Stefan Karlsson from Sweden, Ales Rybka from Czech Republic, Piotr Malowiejski from Poland, Pavel Salnikov from Russia and Yoshihiro Iio from Japan. 
   Currently, the main problem is that we have not been able to add information about Chinese tournament results. So, we are recruiting new members from China.
Requirements: decent to good English level (writing), good knowledge about Chinese tournaments
Tasks: inserting information about Chinese tournaments to renju.net (an admin account will be made for this), actively taking part in discussions.
