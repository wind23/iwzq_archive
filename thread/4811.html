五子棋新闻

#1 Asian Championship forgotten?(亚洲杯被忘记了？) 作者：金枝玉叶 发表时间：2008-10-21 16:19:14

原文：

One of the biggest RIF title competitions, Asian Championship, is supposed to take place in China in this month. Today is October 20, and I have not heard anything from organizers. There has not been any announcement about the tournament location, dates, conditions, etc. Usually such details are announced 4-12 months before the event. Also, there has been no information about giving up or postponing the hosting of the event. Perhaps some other channels than RIF are used for spreading the necessary information that I am not aware of? If so, could anyone please let me know more about the situation of Asian Championship, so that I could update Renjunet accordingly. Thank you!

I feel really worried, because it is the first time in RIF 20 year history when an important title competition was just "forgotten" by organizers. Maybe we are living in more chaotic world than before?

译文：

    RIF大型比赛之一，应该这个月在中国举办。今天是10月20日，但是我没有从组织者那里听到任何消息。没有任何关于这项比赛的地点、日程、具体情况。往常这些细节都是在比赛举办的4-12个月前公布。还有，到目前为止，没有任何消息关于组织者放弃或者打算延期举办这项赛事。或许别的我没听说过的渠道（非RIF）用来传播相关的信息？如果这样，谁能告诉我更多的关于亚洲杯的信息，这样我可以根据这些来更新renju.net网站。谢谢！
    我感到很担忧，因为这是RIF组织成立20年以来第一次出现这么大型的赛事被组织者“忘记”。可能我们现在生活在一个比以前更秩序混乱的世界？
