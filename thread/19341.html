#1 Trig Art 作者：有志青年 发表时间：2013-3-28 17:31:52

Using three arms, sine, and cosine, this project creates beautiful art. Hit space to change backgrounds, c to clear, and h to hide the arms. You can change the rotation speed of any of the arms to create different artwork. ©Firedrake969 2013, some rights reserved. FEATURED! 3/25/13
