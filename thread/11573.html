THE GOMOKU AND RENJU WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS 2011
1. THE GOMOKU AND RENJU WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS 2011
will be held in Sweden in Huskvarna, which is the home town of RIF President Peter Jonsson. Huskvarna is also celebrating 100 years as a city next year.

Time: 1 - 10 August 2011

A more detailed program for the tournaments will be announced within 1-2 weeks.
However, we already know that a free simple type of accomodation will be available. We'll later announce costs for other standard types of accomodation as youth hostels and hotels.
A web site will be launched soon as well.
Until further information is available, please, contact bengt.asplund@gmail.com for any urgent questions regarding the Championships in Sweden.

2. The next TEAM WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP IN RENJU till be held in China in the spring of 2012
