#18 Re:未来规则的讨论 作者：欧艾沃 发表时间：2011-5-27 20:16:50

Ok, I will use English then. I willonce again try to explain Soosyrv-10 and Taraguchi-10 rules

Soosyrv-10:

-The first player puts one of the 26openings.
- The other player has the right to swap.
- The white player puts the 4th move anywhere on the board and declares howmany fifth moves should be played (from 1 to 10).
- The other player has a right to swap.
- The black player puts as many 5th moves on the board as it was declaredbefore. The fifth moves cannot be symmetrical.
- The white player chooses one 5th from these offerings and plays the 6th move.
After that the opening stage is over and the game continues normally by renjurules. 

Taraguchi-10:

- The black player puts the first move in the centreof the board
- The other player has the right to swap.
- The white player will put the 2nd move within3x3 central square.
- The other player has the right to swap.
- The black player will put the 3rd move within5x5 central square.
- The other player has the right to swap..
- The white player will put the 4th anywhere onthe board.
Then choice 1 (after the 4th move):
- The other player has the right to swap.
- The black player will put the 5th anywhere onthe board.
- The other player has the right to swap.
- The white player plays the 6th move anywhere onboard.
The choice 2 (after the 4th move):
- The black player puts ten 5th moves anywhere onboard. The fifth moves cannot be symmetrical.
- The white player chooses one of the offered 5thmoves and plays the 6th move anywhere on board.
After that the opening stage is over and the gamecontinues normally by renju rules.

These 2 rules are actually quite similarto each other. However, there is one big difference. For example, after theopening in Soosyrv-10, I can put a very crazy or a losing 4t

#19 Re:未来规则的讨论 作者：踵酃 发表时间：2011-5-27 20:46:29

简洁翻译版本
soosyrv-10

1。黑方在棋盘上摆出职业26种开局的一种
2。白方有权利选择是否交换
3。持白那方可以在棋盘上的任何交叉点选择第4手，并说出第5手所需要的N打（1打-10都可以）
4。另一方有权交换
5。黑方第5手要摆出之前所决定的N打，不能对称
6。白方选择一个之前黑第5手给出的N打，然后下第6手
7。开局完成，后面正常行棋。


by Dikune

#20 Re:未来规则的讨论 作者：欧艾沃 发表时间：2011-5-27 22:21:43

对，交换是大的问题。日本人真的不喜欢如果有很多交换，他们不明白为什么这是主要。已经2次交换对他们太多。我不知道好的办法，怎么可以相抵白色和黑色开局的时候。From my point of view, no matter how you look at it, one side can always dominate the opening stage if he/she wants to. With old RIF, if I want to make a draw, I just open D11 and no matter how strong my opponent is, if I know theory well enough then my chances of getting a draw are quite good. In Taraguchi rule, the second player can swap until the 4th move, if the opponent makes a weak 4th move, then one can put five 5th moves. I can also put five 5th moves if I want to force a draw, after most 4th moves black has at least 5 playable 5th moves. That is why Taraguchi-10 creates a lot more playable variants. I already explained the minus of Soosyrv-10 rule. In Sakata rule, player 2 can prepare a 4th and 5th move for every opening.
