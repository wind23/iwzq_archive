SERGEY CHERNOV PASSED AWAY
The first Gomoku world champion Sergey Chernov (12.06.1962-16.02.2012) passed away after a heart attack. Sergey Chernov was the best Gomoku player in the world during 80ths. He was a great master of forced wins and a wonderful, good person. His bright memory will live with us.

谢尔盖·切尔诺夫去世

第一届五目世界冠军谢尔盖·切尔诺夫(12.06.1962-16.02.2012)因心脏病发作去世。谢尔盖·切尔诺夫是八十年代最强的五目棋手。他是一位伟大的大师。他将永远活在我们心中。
