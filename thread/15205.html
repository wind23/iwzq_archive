#1 Discussion - Top 5 renju players in the world 作者：欧艾沃 发表时间：2011-9-2 4:11:45

My top 5:
1. Cao Dong - has basically won all there is to win. I hope that now he has more time to analyze and playe renju since he became a renju teacher. 
2. Vladimir Sushkov - it is definitely hard to understand his strength when you do not know him well. Actually, he is a complete player - good physical skills, calculation skills, very strong at making analyses, good nerves, so on and so forth. In my opinion, if he had more time (his work is very intense), he would be the number 1. 
3. Dai Xiaohan - Very humble guy. He said that he thinks he his old but I think that his best years are ahead of him (he has only played renju for 8 years, I think it takes at least 10 years to get to a very high level but he is already there). This world championship was very hard for him (first international tournament, 18 games in 10 days etc.) but he still managed to get the 5th place. 
4. Wu Di - Never underestimate the world champion! His first big tournament win in China just showed that when he is motivated, he can still beat the shit out of everybody. In his case, it is the same with Sushkov: if he had more time, he would be higher on this list. 
5. Tunnet Taimla - It is a big disgrace how this guy has wasted his talents (last time he seriously played renju was 2003 - what a coincidence, he became the world champion that year!)especially take into consideration that he has the time and money to do it (he has made a very good living by playing poker). Still, you should never underestimate him and he is always capable of winning every single tournament that he enters. 
From the outside looking in: Huang Jinxian and Li Yi - the 2 best young players in China, both showed exceptional results in their first foreign tournaments (Li Yi got the best results on the 1st table in 2010 TWC and Huang got he 3rd place in 2011 AT). Still, I would like to see them getting a top 3 result in an individual important Chinese tournament first (also, they are quite busy as I have understood, Li Yi with his work and Huang with his university studies). Still, they have all the time in the world to get there, they are already very close at such a yong age!
Honorary mention:
Nakamura Shigeru: I talked with the current meijin, Oosumi, and he told me that renju is only in 6th place in his life (I think it was something like this: women, going to hot springs, gambling, beer, I forgot the last thing)! So, that he is able to show such good results at his age, unbelievable! This truly makes him a living legend.
